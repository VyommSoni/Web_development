  • ChickenChilli

    • Ingredients

    Boneless chicken (250–300 g), cut into small pieces Cornflour – 2 tbsp All-purpose flour (maida) – 2 tbsp Salt – to taste Black pepper – ½ tsp Ginger-garlic paste – 1 tsp Soy sauce – 1 tbsp Red chilli sauce – 1 tbsp Capsicum (bell pepper), sliced Onion, sliced Green chillies, chopped Garlic, finely chopped Spring onions – for garnish Oil – for frying

    Steps..

    1--Marinate chicken with cornflour, maida, salt, pepper, ginger-garlic paste, and 1 tsp soy sauce. Rest 15 minutes.
    2--Deep-fry or shallow-fry chicken pieces until golden and crispy. Keep aside.
    3-- Heat oil in a pan; sauté garlic, green chillies, and onions.
    4-- Add capsicum and stir-fry on high flame for 1–2 minutes.
    5--Add soy sauce and red chilli sauce; mix well.
    6--Add fried chicken and toss to coat evenly.
    7--Adjust salt and spice if needed.
    8--Garnish with chopped spring onions.
    9--Serve hot with fried rice or noodles.

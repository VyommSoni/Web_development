Chicken Chilli

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken – 300g
  • Cornflour – 2 tbsp
  • Maida – 2 tbsp
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Ginger-garlic paste – 1 tsp
  • Soy sauce – 1 tbsp
  • Red chilli sauce – 1 tbsp
  • Capsicum & Onion

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken for 15 minutes.
  2. Fry until crispy.
  3. Saute vegetables.
  4. Add sauces and mix chicken.
  5. Garnish and serve hot.

Biryani

Ingredients

  • Rice – 2 cups
  • Chicken – 500g
  • Spices & onions

Steps

  1. Cook rice separately.
  2. Cook chicken with spices.
  3. Layer rice and chicken.
  4. Cook on low flame for 10 minutes.

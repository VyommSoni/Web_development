Chicken Chilli

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken – 250g
  • Cornflour – 2 tbsp
  • Soy sauce – 1 tbsp
  • Onion & Capsicum

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken.
  2. Fry until golden.
  3. Cook vegetables with sauce.
  4. Mix and serve.
